# Active Projects & Business Ideas

## Aquifer-Watch / West Texas Water & Data Center Intelligence Dashboard

Civic tech dashboard tracking water usage, aquifer levels, and data center development across West Texas. Built in one day with Claude Code, deployed live on Railway.

Live URL: accomplished-respect-production-29c4.up.railway.app

Key framing: NOT anti-data-center. Pro-transparency, pro-smart-growth. Jack has a personal connection to a Lubbock City Council member (District 4).

Features: Interactive dark Leaflet map with 4,660+ real TWDB groundwater wells, 5 tracked data center sites (Galaxy Helios, Fermi "Project Matador", Calvano/Texas Solarworks, Outlaw Ventures, TeraWulf/Fluidstack), ERCOT fuel breakdown, water impact calculator.

Data sources: TWDB ArcGIS FeatureServer, TWDB bulk download, ERCOT interconnection queue via gridstatus, TCEQ permits, county appraisal data.

Tech: Python FastAPI, vanilla HTML/JS/Leaflet, PostgreSQL with PostGIS, TimescaleDB hypertables, Railway.
